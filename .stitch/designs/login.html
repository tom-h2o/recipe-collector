Honey-Glazed Heirloom Carrots

A vibrant side dish using ancient garden varieties and raw forest honey.

Prep 10m
Cook 25m
Cal 142
Level Easy
Servings
4
language

The Ingredients

Heirloom carrots (bunched)
2 lbs
Extra virgin olive oil
3 tbsp
Raw forest honey
2 tbsp
Fresh thyme sprigs
4-5

Instructions

1

Prepare the Garden Harvest

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Scrub the heirloom carrots thoroughly but leave the skin on to preserve the earthy nutrients and rustic texture. Trim the tops, leaving about an inch of green.

2

The Glaze Emulsion

In a small copper bowl, whisk together the olive oil, forest honey, and salt. AI Tip: If your honey is too thick, warm it slightly over a candle-lit flame for a perfect pour.

3

Roast & Infuse

Toss the carrots with the glaze on a heavy baking sheet. Spread them in an asymmetric single layer and tuck the thyme sprigs between them. Roast for 20-25 minutes until the tips are slightly charred.
